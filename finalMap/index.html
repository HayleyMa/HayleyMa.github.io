Worth It

An online food show where two presenters, Steven Lim and Andrew Ilnyckyj, and their camera man, Adam Bianchi, try a variety of different cuisine at different price points (low, moderate, and high). At the end of each episode, the hosts deem which restaurant was the most "Worth It" in their opinion out of the three restaurants.
